Cyber Security Community Bina Nusantara

About Us

Abbreviated as CSC, an Student Activity Unit created by BINUS University students who have interest and concern on Cyber Security. Cyber Security Community is a Student Activity Unit to learn together, share knowledge and experiences about Cyber Security.

VISION

To realize solidarity, kinship, and accountability in organization, thus allowing its member to optimize knowledge of cybersecurity, and become an organization that is well known and has good relationships with companies and institutions in 2020.

MISSION

To gather BINUS University students who are interested in security, to make activities which strengthen kinship in organizations, to deepen knowledge by learning about cybersecurity, to actively participate in events and competitions related to Cyber Security, and to establish good relationships with companies and institutions.
